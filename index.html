Special Circumstances Day

A Celebration of the Genius of Iain (M.) Banks

Let’s all celebrate the geniusness of our favourite author Iain (M.) Banks, now, while he is still among us!

When?

June 28 (a friday in 2013) – This is the day on which two of the main storylines of Walking on Glass are set (in 1983). This day has some precedence, as it has been celebrated for several years by a small number of people (as Graham Park Day), by retracing the steps of Graham to Half Moon Crescent.

How?

This has yet to be decided. Help us by joining the discussion on Facebook or Iain [M] Banks Fan Forum.

I think we should carry around some marker of our tribute for the day (think Towel Day). But we still need something (like a towel, but different) to show our participation in this magnificent event – something to set the fans of Banks aside from the crowd for one day each year – but how? We need your ideas!

How Can I Spread the Word?

Tell everyone you know! Especially Iain (M.) Banks fans! Let’s make this a recurring yearly celebration!

  • Like us on Facebook, and tell your friends to do the same!
  • Twitter using hashtag #SpecialCircumstancesDay
  • Join our discussion/brainstorm on this iainbanksforum.net discussion thread.
